As part of the Weaving Threads of Change: Transformative Responses to Gender-Based Violence research project, Vivic Research, the Centre for Resilience and Social Development (CRSD) and the Ottawa Coalition to End Violence Against Women (OCTEVAW) worked with illustrator and artist cslackdesign to produce a zine with the research findings. This zine centers the experiences that community members shared during the research process when discussing their experiences simultaneously navigating gender-based violence and the immigration system.
