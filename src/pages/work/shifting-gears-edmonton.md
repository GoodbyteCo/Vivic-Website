Vivic Research's economist Inez Hillel authored a report for Parkland Institute on the increasingly challenging financial situation faced by the City of Edmonton. This report explores how the frayed relationship between the province and the municipality stunts financial possibilities, with city workers and Edmontonians ultimately bearing the cost of this decline. The research process combined interviews and an in-depth review of municipal and provincial funding documents and puts forward a range of solutions to address fiscal strain, improve management-labour relations, and transparency with the public.
